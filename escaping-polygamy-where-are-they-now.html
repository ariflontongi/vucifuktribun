Escaping Polygamy Where Are They Now?

Introduction to Escaping Polygamy

“Escaping Polygamy” is a documentary series that premiered on Lifetime in 2014. The show follows the work of three sisters – Jessica, Andrea, and Shanell – who left the Kingston Clan, a polygamous group also known as the Order, based in Salt Lake City, Utah. The series showcases their efforts to help others break free from polygamous sects and start new lives. Over the years, the show has gained attention for shedding light on the secretive and often oppressive world of polygamous cults. But what has become of those who bravely stepped forward to leave their communities and share their stories with the world?

Where Are They Now?

Since the show’s inception, many viewers have become invested in the lives of those who have left their polygamous groups. The question “Where are they now?” is a common one among fans of the series. Here, we delve into the lives of some of the most memorable individuals featured on “Escaping Polygamy” and provide updates on their journeys.

Life After the Show

For many participants, life after “Escaping Polygamy” has been a journey of self-discovery and healing. They have had to navigate the challenges of integrating into mainstream society, often dealing with trauma and the complexities of building new relationships while severing old ones.

Continued Activism and Advocacy

Some of the individuals who have left their polygamous communities have become outspoken activists and advocates for others in similar situations. They use their experiences to educate the public and provide support to those seeking to escape polygamy.

Personal Growth and Education

Many escapees have focused on their education, pursuing college degrees or vocational training to build a foundation for their new lives. Education has been a critical step for these individuals, as it was often discouraged or limited in their former communities.

Family Dynamics

Rebuilding or maintaining family relationships has been a complex issue for many who have left polygamous sects. Some have successfully reconnected with family members who have also left, while others have had to come to terms with the loss of those still involved in polygamous practices.

Challenges and Setbacks

Not all stories have had happy endings. Some individuals have faced significant challenges, including legal battles, custody disputes, and financial hardships. The process of escaping and adjusting to a new way of life can be fraught with obstacles.

Success Stories

Despite the challenges, there are numerous success stories of individuals who have built fulfilling lives post-escape. They have found new careers, started families, and become pillars in their communities, demonstrating resilience and determination.

Updates on Specific Individuals

While it’s not possible to cover every person featured on the show, here are updates on a few of the individuals who made a lasting impression on viewers.

Jessica, Andrea, and Shanell

The three sisters who were central to the series have continued their mission to help others leave polygamous groups. They have also been involved in various advocacy projects and public speaking engagements to raise awareness about the issues surrounding polygamy.

Priscilla

One of the most heart-wrenching stories was that of Priscilla, who left the FLDS with her children. She has since been working to provide a stable and loving home for her family, while also pursuing educational opportunities.

Rachel

Rachel’s escape was particularly dramatic, and her journey afterward has been one of empowerment. She has been focused on her education and advocating for others who have left or are looking to leave polygamous communities.

Shanell’s Daughter

Shanell’s daughter, who made the brave decision to leave with her mother, has been adjusting to life outside the Order. She has been pursuing her education and working on building a new life for herself.

Impact of the Show

“Escaping Polygamy” has had a significant impact on its viewers and participants alike. It has brought attention to the often-hidden issues within polygamous communities and has provided a platform for those seeking to leave.

Raising Awareness

The show has played a crucial role in raising awareness about the realities of life in polygamous sects, including the lack of personal freedom, the exploitation of women and children, and the challenges faced by those who leave.

Support Networks

As a result of the show, there has been an increase in support networks and resources for individuals escaping polygamy. This includes safe houses, legal assistance, and counseling services.

Changing Perceptions

“Escaping Polygamy” has also helped change public perceptions of polygamy, highlighting the distinction between consensual polyamorous relationships and coercive polygamous cults.

Legal and Social Challenges

The legal and social challenges faced by those leaving polygamous communities are significant. They often have to navigate complex legal systems to gain independence and custody of their children.

Custody Battles

Many escapees face custody battles as they seek to protect their children from the polygamous lifestyle. These legal fights can be lengthy and emotionally draining.

Identity and Documentation Issues

Some individuals from polygamous sects lack proper identification and documentation, making it difficult for them to access services and integrate into society.

Financial Independence

Achieving financial independence is another hurdle for those leaving polygamy. They often start with little to no financial resources and must learn to manage their finances for the first time.

Continued Support and Resources

Support and resources for those escaping polygamy continue to grow, thanks in part to the increased visibility from the show.

Non-Profit Organizations

Non-profit organizations dedicated to helping individuals escape from polygamous groups have been established, providing a range of services from emotional support to practical assistance.

Community Outreach

Community outreach programs have been developed to educate the public and offer resources to those in need. These programs also work to prevent the isolation often experienced by those who leave polygamous communities.

Online Support Groups

Online support groups have become a vital resource for individuals seeking advice and camaraderie with others who have had similar experiences.

FAQ Section

  • What happened to the original cast members of “Escaping Polygamy”?
    The original cast members, Jessica, Andrea, and Shanell, have continued their advocacy work and remain committed to helping others leave polygamous groups.
  • Are there any resources available for people wanting to escape polygamy?
    Yes, there are several non-profit organizations, support groups, and community outreach programs available to assist those looking to escape polygamous communities.
  • Has “Escaping Polygamy” led to any legal changes regarding polygamous groups?
    While the show has raised awareness and sparked conversations, legal changes are complex and slow-moving. The show has, however, highlighted the need for better laws and enforcement to protect individuals in these communities.

Conclusion

“Escaping Polygamy” has provided a unique glimpse into the lives of individuals seeking freedom from oppressive polygamous sects. The show has not only raised awareness but has also inspired action and support for those in need. While the journey for many has been fraught with challenges, the resilience and courage displayed by the escapees continue to inspire and effect change. As we look at where they are now, it’s clear that the legacy of “Escaping Polygamy” is one of hope, transformation, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.
