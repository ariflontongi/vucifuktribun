David Zayas Net Worth

David Zayas is a Puerto Rican actor born on August 15, 1962 in Ponce, PR. He is best known for his role as Sgt. Angel Batista on Showtime's thriller series Dexter. He has also appeared in films such as The Expendables and Oz, as well as television shows such as Law & Order and Blue Bloods.
