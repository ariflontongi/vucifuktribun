Fall Out Boy's Patrick Stump is now a dad

Fall Out Boy's lead singer Patrick Stump is now a proud parent. Photo / AP

Patrick Stump has become a father for the first time.

The Fall Out Boy rocker announced last month he and wife Elisa Yao were expecting their first child together and now Ellen DeGeneres - whose talk show he will appear on in a few weeks time - has revealed the couple have welcomed son Declan into the world.

She tweeted alongside a picture of the baby: "It's a boy! Actually, it's a @FallOutBoy.
"Congrats @PatrickStump and welcome baby Declan! See y'all in 2 weeks! (sic)"

It’s a boy! Actually, it’s a @FallOutBoy. Congrats @PatrickStump and welcome baby Declan! See y’all in 2 weeks! pic.twitter.com/aJ0xZw8Buj

Patrick recently revealed life in the band has been chaotic thanks to his impending new arrival and bandmate Pete Wentz's kids, five-year-old Bronx from his marriage to Ashlee Simpson and eight-week-old Saint with girlfriend Meagan Camper.

He said: ""I'm about to have a kid or a tattoo. I'm like the snows of Siberia - just untouched.
"The band is in a crazy place right now because there's a lot of little kids running around."

And the Dance, Dance singer admitted Pete had been passing on parenting tips.

Asked what he had advised him, Patrick said: "You can never have enough Kleenex and baby wipes."
