Queendom Puzzle Introduces Artist Lineup With Group Teaser And Profiles

“Queendom Puzzle” has introduced its contestants with new profiles!

Mnet’s “Queendom Puzzle” is a spin-off variety show of “Queendom” that will form a powerful global project girl group by taking members from currently promoting girl groups as well as female artists and putting them together like a puzzle.

The lineup of artists includes former CLC member Yeeun, former MOMOLAND member JooE, former BNK48 member Fye (Fyeqoodgurl), Lovelyz’s Kei, Weeekly’s Jihan, Lee Soojin, Soeun, and Zoa, Cherry Bullet’s Jiwon, Bora, and Chaerin, tripleS’s Jiwoo and Yoon Seoyeon, woo!ah!’s Nana and Wooyeon, H1-KEY’s Riina and Hwiseo, LIGHTSUM’s Sangah, WJSN’s Yeoreum, PURPLE KISS’s Yuki, Rocket Punch’s Suyun, Yeonhee, and Takahashi Juri, AOA’s Dohwa (Chanmi), Weki Meki’s Elly, and former “Produce 48” contestant and NMB48 member Shiroma Miru.

Alongside a captivating group photo, showing the artists with MC Girls’ Generation‘s Taeyeon, “Queendom Puzzle” has revealed profiles for each of the 26 contestants. These profiles highlight each artist’s birthday, Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), stats in vocal, rap, dance, and stage presence, epic skills, and keywords.
