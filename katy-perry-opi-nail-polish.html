Katy Perry's OPI Nail Polish is a (Teenage) Dream

Teenage Dream fans take note: There's now a nail polish named for our December cover star, Katy Perry's pop album. It's the fun, glittery pink above, part of the Katy Perry for OPI collection.

Known for her fun, devil-may-care style, Perry's beauty picks are equally adventurous: big lashes, bold lips, serious bangs...and amazing nails. It's no wonder OPI's artistic director, Suzi Weiss-Fischmann, jumped at the chance to work with Perry. Our other favorites from the line are the bonus topcoat, Black Shatter, and the bright berry, The One that Got Away. They're out in January 2011, but get on the waiting list now.

For more of the never-ending fun that is Katy Perry, read the interview, watch the video and flip though our sultry cover shoot.
