Why Choose SOLS 24/7 Malaysia?

SOLS 24/7 Malaysia, founded in 2000, is a non-profit organisation committed in serving, educating, and empowering less privileged communities through providing quality education and social empowerment programs. Through programs funded and supported by our partners and founders, we are able to grant free education in six countries - Cambodia, India, Laos, Malaysia, Thailand, and Timor Leste. Our organisation envisions a free access to education from all communities regardless of race, religion or gender. Over the years, SOLS 24/7 has been able to train students, establish training and boarding centres, and gained numerous full-time team members in more than 50 countries. We have received awards and international recognitions inclined to education and development projects.
