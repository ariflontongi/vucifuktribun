Glow in Red: Ini Edo, Hilda Baci, 4 Others Give Style Inspiration for Valentine's Day

  • Valentine's is the season of love often celebrated with red, and some celebs have shown what it looks like to glow in the colour
  • Hilda Baci and Ini Edo, among other celebs, have given their fans some style inspiration on how to rock red
  • In this listicle, Legit.ng outlines how one can turn up in different designs in red, turn heads, and still look comfortable

Red is a beautiful colour, and many Nigerian celebrities have been caught slaying in it.

In the spirit of Valentine's Day on February 14, celebrity chef Hilda Baci, actresses Ini Edo, Regina Daniels, and Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde, among others, have shown off how ladies can turn up for the day of love.

These female celebs blended their outfits with some accessories to make them appear more glamorous. Legit.ng has gathered a list of celebrities in breathtaking red outfits. Let's take a look at them below.
