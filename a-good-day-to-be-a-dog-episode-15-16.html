A Good Day to Be a Dog Episode 15 &amp; 16 Release Date, Spoiler, Storylines, Characters &amp; Trailer

A Good Day to Be a Dog Episode 15 & 16: A Good Day to Be a Dog is a popular South Korean drama series that is based on a webtoon of the same name by Lee Hey. The show stars Cha Eun-woo, Park Gyu-young, Lee Hyun-woo, and Yoon Hyun-soo in the lead roles. The show follows the story of Han Hae-na, a high school teacher who turns into a dog when she kisses someone, and Jin Seo-won, a math teacher who is afraid of dogs. The show is a mix of romance, comedy, and fantasy that has captivated the viewers with its unique plot and charming characters.

The show premiered on October 11, 2023, on MBC TV and has been airing every Wednesday at 9 p.m. KST. The show has a total of 14 episodes, each lasting about an hour. The show is also available to stream on Viki and Viu for the global audience.

Storylines and Plot

The show has several storylines that are intertwined with the main plot. Here are some of the storylines that have been developed throughout the show:

  • The love triangle between Han Hae-na, Jin Seo-won, and Lee Bo-gyeom. Lee Bo-gyeom is a history teacher who has a crush on Han Hae-na. He is kind, caring, and handsome, but he is also jealous and possessive. He tries to win Han Hae-na’s heart, but he is unaware of her curse and her feelings for Jin Seo-won. He also has a rivalry with Jin Seo-won, who is his childhood friend and his opposite in personality. Jin Seo-won is a math teacher who is cold, aloof, and arrogant, but he is also smart, handsome, and loyal. He falls in love with Han Hae-na, but he is afraid of dogs and does not know how to express his emotions. He also has a traumatic past that haunts him.
  • The friendship between Han Hae-na and Yoon Chae-ah. Yoon Chae-ah is an English teacher who is Han Hae-na’s best friend. She is cheerful, bubbly, and supportive, but she is also insecure and lonely. She has a crush on Choi Yul, a music teacher who is handsome, talented, and popular, but he is also arrogant, rude, and insensitive. He does not reciprocate her feelings and often ignores her. She tries to impress him, but she also gets hurt by him.
  • The romance between Han Yu-na and Song Woo-taek. Han Yu-na is Han Hae-na’s younger sister who is a college student. She is rebellious, spoiled, and immature, but she is also cute, lively, and honest. She has a crush on Song Woo-taek, a webtoon artist who is the older brother of Song Da-eun, Han Hae-na’s student. He is cool, handsome, and creative, but he is also shy, introverted, and awkward. He likes Han Yu-na, but he does not know how to approach her. He also has a secret that he does not want anyone to know.
  • The mystery behind the curse. The curse that affects Han Hae-na and her family has been passed down for generations. The origin of the curse is unknown, but it is said that it was caused by a witch who was betrayed by her lover. The curse makes the women in the family turn into a dog when they kiss someone, and the only way to break it is to kiss the same person again while they are a dog. The curse also has some side effects, such as making the women more attractive, more fertile, and more loyal. The curse also affects the men who kiss them, making them more protective, more possessive, and more obsessed.
