Nick Aldis Touts Relationship With Steiner Family After Bron Breakker SmackDown Signing

Nick Aldis stepped into the role of SmackDown General Manager in October of the previous year, and his performance has been well-received by the audience since then. This week, he brought Bron Breakker into the Friday Night SmackDown roster and highlighted his connection with the Steiner family following the signing.

As seen on this week’s episode of Friday Night SmackDown, Nick Aldis emerged at ringside to announce his ongoing mission of signing top talent for the WWE Universe to witness weekly.

Bron Breakker revealed that after weeks of negotiations, Aldis successfully secured one of the hottest free agents. Subsequently, Bron Breakker entered the ring, greeted Nick Aldis, and officially signed the SmackDown contract.

Following his massive acquisition, Nick Aldis took to Twitter and uploaded photos of himself alongside Bron Breakker’s uncle Scott Steiner, as Aldis and Steiner have worked together in promotions such as TNA and more. He reflected that he maintains a good relationship with the Steiner family.

”How it started —> How it’s going”

We will have to wait and see how Bron Breakker will be booked on Friday Night SmackDown, as he is still one half of the NXT Tag Team Champions and has an obligation there as well.
