What is the largest bowling alley in the United States?

published on August 31, 2024
Answer The Thunderbowl in Allen Park, Michigan, which opened in 1962 and has hosted the Professional Bowlers Association World Series of Bowling, is the world’s biggest bowling alley. It has 90 lanes and has been in operation since 1962. Both the Stardust Bowl in Addison, Illinois, and the Freeway Lanes in Wickliffe, Ohio, have 84 lanes apiece.   People frequently inquire as to where the world’s biggest bowling alley might be found.
