Salaries In Nigeria - Legit.ng

The latest survey, provided by SalarySurvey, shows the statistics of salaries in Nigeria. Check the most highly-paid jobs, as well as average salary of the Nigerian citizens.

According to the study there are 23 job categories with different salary rates.

According to the estimates, the average yearly salary in Nigeria is 658,324 NGN (USD3,596), with the maximum of 5,000,000 NGN (USD27,320) and the minimum of 37,000 NGN (USD202).

It should be noted that there is a real problem with salaries and their timely payment in Nigeria.
