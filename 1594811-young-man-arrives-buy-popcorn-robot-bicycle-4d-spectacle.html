Living in 3034: Young Man Arrives to Buy Popcorn on His Robot-Like Bicycle and 4D Spectacle

  • A young man who arrived to buy popcorn on his robot-like bicycle wearing 4D spectacles gained attention online
  • In the video, the young man departed after purchasing his popcorn on the electric bicycle, still wearing his 4D spectacles
  • Although the video itself did not initially attract much attention, someone behind the camera captured the moment

A young man attracted online attention when he showed up to buy popcorn on a high-tech bicycle, wearing 4D spectacles.

The video showed him riding away on his electric bike with his popcorn in hand, still sporting the unusual glasses.

While the video didn't initially gain much traction, a bystander captured the scene and uploaded it to TikTok, where it started to get noticed, as shown by @whistle.
