Who is Femi Otedola?

Femi Otedola is a Nigerian businessman and philanthropist who has made a significant impact in various sectors, primarily in energy. He is the chairman of Forte Oil PLC, an importer of fuel products, and the founder of Zenon Petroleum and Gas Ltd. His business ventures extend beyond oil and gas, touching sectors such as finance, real estate, and shipping.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Otedola’s journey to wealth began early in his life. Born into a well-known family, his father was the former governor of Lagos State. Despite his privileged background, Otedola made a name for himself through his entrepreneurial spirit and business acumen. He started his career in the family’s printing business before venturing into the oil and gas industry.

The Rise of Zenon Petroleum and Gas

In 2003, Otedola established Zenon Petroleum and Gas Ltd, which quickly became a major player in the Nigerian petroleum industry. Zenon controlled a significant share of the diesel market, supplying fuel to a vast majority of the country’s major corporations, including Dangote Group, MTN, Unilever, and more.

Forte Oil PLC and Expansion

Otedola’s acquisition of a controlling stake in African Petroleum, which he later rebranded as Forte Oil PLC, marked a significant expansion of his business empire. Under his leadership, Forte Oil grew to become one of the largest oil distribution and retail companies in Nigeria, with a substantial presence in the power sector as well.

Diversification of Investments

Understanding that diversification is key to maintaining wealth, Otedola has invested in a range of sectors. His investments include real estate across Nigeria, the United States, and the United Kingdom, as well as financial institutions and technology companies.
