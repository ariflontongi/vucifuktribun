  • Denise Miller-Jonas, also known as “Mama Jonas,” is an American former teacher and a beloved celebrity.
  • She is widely recognized as the mother of the famous Jonas Brothers, the American Pop Rock band.
  • Let’s delve into some fascinating details about Denise Miller-Jonas, her family, her personal life, and her connection with the Jonas Brothers and Priyanka Chopra.

Jonas and the Jonas Brothers

  • Denise’s sons, Kevin, Joe, Nick, and Frankie Jonas, rose to fame through the Disney show “Jonas.”
  • Among them, Nick Jonas, the original founder of the band, is especially popular.
  • On 18th August 2018, Nick Jonas got engaged to Bollywood star Priyanka Chopra, and Denise and her husband blessed the couple.
  • Priyanka’s mother, Madhu Chopra, was also present at the engagement ceremony in Mumbai.

Age, Family, and Ethnicity

  • Denise was born on 12th July 1966 in Dallas, Texas, making her 52 years old as of 2018.
  • She shares a close bond with her mother, Frances Madonia-Miller.
  • However, there is limited information available about her father and siblings.
  • Denise belongs to Caucasian-White ethnicity.

Marital Life and Children

  • Denise tied the knot with Paul Kevin Jonas Sr. on 15th August 1985.
  • They welcomed their first son, Kevin Jonas, on 5th November 1987, who is now married to Danielle Jonas and has two daughters.
  • Their second son, Joe Jonas, was born on 15th August 1989, and after three years, they welcomed their third son, Nick Jonas, in 1992.
  • Later, their fourth son, Frankie Jonas, was born on 28th September 2000.

Education and Hobbies

Net Worth

Interesting Facts about Mama Jonas

Conclusion
