Harry Styles' unexpected response to a fan who asked him if he wanted to have sex during concert

Harry Styles has sent social media into meltdown after seemingly flirting with a fan who asked him, via a sign in the crowd, if he wanted to 'shag'.

The British superstar was performing in the Welsh city Cardiff when he spotted the cheeky sign amongst the thousands of people present at the city's Principality Stadium.

In the viral video of the reaction between Harry and one of his followers, Harry appears to smile and nod as if to say 'yes' when he sees the cheeky invitation.

Other concertgoers can be heard screaming in the background in a crazed frenzy after noticing Harry's reaciton and the clip has also caused quite a noise on social media.

Thousands of users commented on the clip to express their delight and jealousy. "Harry's eye contact. I would die," said one user. "He basically said, 'really? Ok!'' said another.
