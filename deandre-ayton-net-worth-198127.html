DeAndre Ayton Net Worth

DeAndre Ayton is a center who was born on July 23, 1998 in Nassau, Bahamas. He joined the University of Arizona Wildcats as a five-star recruit in 2017 after a successful high school career at Hillcrest Prep Academy in Phoenix, Arizona, where he averaged 26 points and 15 rebounds. He was also selected to play in the Jordan Brand Classic and McDonald's All-American Game.

About

Center who joined the University of Arizona Wildcats as a five-star recruit in 2017. He played in the Jordan Brand Classic and McDonald's All-American Game after averaging 26 points and 15 rebounds at Hillcrest Prep Academy in Phoenix, Arizona. 

Before Fame

He grew up playing the tuba and was scouted by The All Bahamian Brand before he began playing basketball. He later moved to San Diego, California. 

Trivia

He represented the Bahamas at 2016 Centrobasket, where he led the tournament in rebounds per game. 

Family Life

His parents are Alvin and Andrea Ayton. He has four siblings. 

Associated With

He has played alongside Chase Jeter at the University of Arizona.
