Kap G Biography, Girlfriend, Height, Age, Net Worth, Brother, Parents

Kap G, born George Ramirez, was inspired to forge a path for himself in music after being bullied as a child. Although his parents didn’t make that decision at first, they later gave him their blessing after they were convinced that music was his forte. Kap G started his career by joining a group of teenagers known as “Kids At Play” (KAP). It was while with the team that he came up with his stage name – a combination of Kap and his initial ‘G’. Today, the musician is one of the hottest rappers in the world. After releasing several singles, he shared the international stage with notable musical elites.

Kap G’s Bio (Age)

George Ramirez was born to Mexican parents in College Park, Georgia on July 31, 1994. He was raised with his siblings in Metro Atlanta, Georgia, United States. He went to high school at Tri-Cities High School, where he started freestyling with instruments such as Yung LA and Gucci Mane.

In an interview, Kap G said that while growing up, his brothers hit the clock in the warehouses, but he knew he was born to survive behind the mic, standing on the stage. The rapper’s breakthrough was the hit ‘Tatted Like Amigos’, released in 2012 and fronted by famed rapper/record producer, Chief Keef (born Keith Farrelle Cozart). The collaboration has since spawned several mixtapes, namely: Like A Mexican (2014), Real Migo Shit 2 (2014), Real Migo Shit 3 (2015), El Southside (2016), and Real Migo Shit 4 (2018); as well as two studio albums – SupaJefe (2017) and No Kap (2018). He also has an EP titled Mood, which was released in 2017.

During his burgeoning career, the Mexican-American rap star has teamed up with popular artists such as Gunna, David Banner, Pharrell Williams, Young Thug, Baeza, Trinidad James, and GT Garza.

Apart from being a phenomenal rapper, Kap G is also an actor. In 2015, he had a guest appearance in the coming-of-age comedy-drama film, Dope. George has been featured many times in numerous ‘XXL’ magazine articles. He was nominated for XXL Freshman in 2015 and was later included in their annual freshman class on June 12, 2017, featuring artists such as XXXTentacion, Ugly God, Playboi Carti, A Hooded Boogie, MadeinTYO, Kamaiyah, PnB Rock , KYLE and Amine.

His net worth

Being a talent who knows how to create a sensation with his awesome collaborations and great music, Kap G has the opportunity to become one of the most lucrative rappers in the world in the near future. It’s because he’s creative, focused, passionate about music and has everything it takes to profit hugely from the music industry. Currently, he is worth $500,000, according to sources. He makes his money from his music career and other projects.

Parents and Brother

George’s parents are from San Marcos, Mexico. Although their names and other personal details are not known to the public, the two are said to have moved to the United States in the late 1980s. Kap G’s mother worked as a maid while his father drove tractor-trailers and was doing construction work. The youngest of six siblings, the rapper has one sister and four brothers whose identities have yet to be publicly revealed.

According to sources, George owes his rise to the prominence of one of his brothers who associated him with the acclaimed rapper/record producer Chef Keef (born Keith Farrelle Cozart). His brother learned of Keef’s existence after a Chicago barber introduced him to Keef’s music. Kap G’s brother quickly tracked him down and told him his brother was good. Keef would agree to record a guest verse for Kag G’s song, after which American record executive Kawan Prather was drawn to the song. Kawan later became his manager and also helped him sign a deal with Atlantic Records label.
