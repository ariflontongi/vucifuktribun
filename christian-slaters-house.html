Inside Christian Slaters Miami Home - recently listed for $3.95 million

Christian Slaterâ€™s Coconut Grove home has just been listed on the market with a price tag of $3.95 million. 

Slater is an American actor and producer best known for his roles in the hit TV shows West Wing and Mr. Robot having made his big-screen debut in The Legend of Billie Jean in 1985. 

Whilst the 52-year-old New York-born star has become known as a Hollywood bad boy, his Florida home is a picture of Spanish elegance. Here, we explore the property that is perfectly nestled among some of the world's best homes. 

The four-bedroom, three and half-bath home spanning 3,370-square feet of space is an Italian-inspired dream home. Also called "Villa Dolce Far Niente", the house is a meticulously renovated family home situated on a lush, gated street close to the waterfront. 

The first floor of the main house is perfect for entertaining with a living area, fireplace, and den and is the ideal black slate to test out a multitude of decorating ideas.

The house boasts a large dining area that opens up to the outdoor pool space via three custom-arched mahogany doors that are bound to inspire beautiful dining room ideas for the next owner.

Connected to the main dining space is a newly renovated kitchen that boasts appliances by kitchen giants SubZero, Viking, and Miele. This kitchen also comes with a large pantry and breakfast and dining nook area to compliment the main dining area. 

On the second floor lives the remaining three bedrooms and two full bathrooms. The oversized master suite features a dreamy Juliet balcony with views of the pool, a large walk-in closet, and an en-suite marble bathroom with a separate shower and soaking tub. 

Find a gorgeous rooftop terrace with several seating areas and chairs for lounging as well as space to implement some roof garden ideas. 

A detached guest suite can be found beside the main house and is ideal for hosting family or friends. The annex features an entertaining area, bedroom, and full bathroom with a steam shower for the perfect reclusive escape. 

Outdoors, find a large heated sloping pool to try out your pool ideas, as well as a self-contained home generator.

Less than a mile walk into the heart of Coconut Grove to shops and first-class dining; the property is ideal for families as it is surrounded by some of the best private schools Miami has to offer.

Listed for $3.95 million by Liz Hogan and Charles Celesia with Compass.
