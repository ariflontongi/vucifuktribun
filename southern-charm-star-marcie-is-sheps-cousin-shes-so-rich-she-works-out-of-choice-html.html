Southern Charm star Marcie is Sheps cousin, shes so rich she works out of choice

Spread the love             

The beloved cast of Southern Charm returned in 2022, bringing their signature Charleston charm and a fresh wave of dramas revolving around love and friendship. Among the new arrivals is Marcie Hobbs, a cousin to the long-standing star Shep Rose. Let’s delve into Marcie’s background, her family’s affluence, and her choice to work despite her financial security.

A Glimpse into Marcie’s Life

Joining the Southern Charm ensemble in 2022, Marcie Hobbs is none other than Shep Rose’s younger cousin. Shep, a familiar face on Bravo since 2014, welcomes Marcie into the mix. After a life filled with globetrotting adventures, Marcie has decided to call Charleston her home. The embrace of stability also extends to her family life, as she shares the joys of parenthood with her husband John, and together they cherish their young daughter, Edie.

Choosing Passion Over Privilege

Marcie’s Bravo biography unveils a fascinating facet of her life. Though she is blessed with abundant family wealth that negates the need for employment, Marcie opts to channel her energy into a successful career as a real estate agent. This choice reflects her dedication to personal growth and professional achievement.

According to Shep, a significant number of his family members have achieved remarkable success in their respective fields. The roots of their affluence trace back to his grandfather, a titan in the steel industry. On his mother’s side, the family’s legacy is equally illustrious, with ties to the founding of Chicago’s railways. Their association with Lake Forest Chicago, an enclave of opulence, further underscores their prestigious background.

Shep’s mother’s family boasts a lineage deeply entrenched in legal eminence, with both of his siblings practicing law in Charlotte. The opulent narrative of Shep and Marcie’s family continues, with their parents relocating to the exclusive haven of Hilton Head Island, South Carolina, during Shep’s early years.

Unveiling Marcie Hobbs’ Rich Heritage

Marcie Hobbs’ family tree is rooted in prominence. Her father, Dexter Cumming Hobbs, and her mother, Nan Hobbs Barganier, have contributed to the legacy of achievement. Notably, Marcie’s mother garnered The Maury D. Smith Award for Individual Community Leaders in 2020, a testament to her impactful contributions.

Marcie’s cherished family holds a special place in her heart, as evident from her heartfelt tributes on Instagram. In 2020, she paid homage to her late grandmother, Nance, a poignant gesture that resonated deeply with her followers.

As we welcome Marcie into the Southern Charm fold, we gain insight not only into her own journey but also into the tapestry of prosperity and influence that shapes her remarkable family story. Through her choice to pursue a fulfilling career, Marcie exemplifies a blend of privilege and passion that is sure to leave an indelible mark on the Southern Charm legacy.
