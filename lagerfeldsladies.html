Paris and Eva join Karl at champagne-filled launch

    There was only one place to be sipping champagne on Friday night. Paris Hilton, Eva Herzigova and Lindsay Lohan were just some of the sexy stars lured by an evening of bubbly fun in Beverly Hills in honour of Karl Lagerfeld's new photo campaign for Dom Perignon.

    Man of the night Karl was the centre of attention as he posed with the beautiful A-list crowd. Wearing his hallmark leather gloves and dark glasses, the silver-haired designer made a typically flamboyant statement in a ruff-necked shirt, blue bow-tie and sparkly belt. He spent the evening surrounded by a bevy of seductively clad ladies who had all made an effort to look their most stylish in front of the Chanel maestro.

    Paris was elegant in a cream and black frock, Eva showed off her endless legs in a chiffon shift while Lindsay had opted for the seductive, little black dress option. Fellow party-goers included Pierce Brosnan, who was accompanied by his wife Keely, curvaceous in a figure-hugging gown and glittering gold jacket.

    Naturally enough champagne flowed at the party, which was hosted by Karl and supermodel Eva Herzigova at a private home in Beverly Hills, to celebrate the unveiling of the new image of Dom Perignon Rose Vintage 1996. Karl's photo campaign tells the story of strangers - a man and a woman -who cross paths at one of Paris's finest hotels for a romantic encounter.
