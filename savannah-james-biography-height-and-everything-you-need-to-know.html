Savannah James Biography, Height And Everything You Need To Know

American businesswoman and philanthropist Savannah James (née Brinson) was born in America. She was born on August 27, 1986, in Akron, Ohio, USA. She is best recognized for being LeBron James’ wife, an NBA star.

Savannah James Early Life and Education

Ohio’s Akron is where Savannah was born and reared. When she was born, her parents, J.K. and Jennifer Brinson, were still in their teenage years. LeBron James went to the same Akron high school that Savannah did, St. Vincent-St. Mary.

Savannah James Career

The Women’s Foundation of Akron, a nonprofit that supports women and girls in the Akron region through charity and education, was founded by Savannah. She has participated in numerous humanitarian organizations throughout the years and is philanthropic as well.

Savannah James Personal Life

When Savannah and LeBron James first spoke, they were both 16 years old. Soon after, they began dating, and they have been together ever since. On December 31, 2011, the pair announced their engagement. Their wedding took place in San Diego, California, on September 14, 2013. Together, they are parents to Zhuri James, a daughter, LeBron James Jr., and Bryce Maximus James, two sons.

Savannah James Height

Savannah James is 170 centimeters (5 feet 7 inches) tall.
