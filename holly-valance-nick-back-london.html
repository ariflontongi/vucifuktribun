Newlyweds Holly and Nick back in UK ahead of honeymoon

    They had the wedding of their dreams, but the honeymoon will just have to wait for Holly Valance and her new husband Nick Candy.The couple jetted back to London after tying the knot during a lavish ceremony in Beverly Hills.There was no mistaking Holly's just wed glow as she and Nick made their way through arrivals at Heathrow.

    The blonde singer looked on top of the world as she pushed her trolley of luggage along with a demure smile on her lips.Mr and Mrs Candy - who refer to themselves using the combined surname "Valandy" - travelled back with close friends Gary and Danielle Lineker, who also attended the three-day nuptials.The football legend had tweeted earlier: "'Return home today. Thanks so much and many congratulations to Holly and Nick #valandy . A truly spectacular 3 days." singer Holly and her property developer beau are said to have spent a cool £3 million on their fairytale wedding celebrations.

    The largest dent on the bill was the fee for wedding singer Katy Perry, who was reportedly paid £1.2 million to serenade guests such as Sarah, Duchess of York, Simon Cowell and artist Tracy Emin.

    Then there was the bride's cascading £35,000 J'Aton dress complete with an extravagant train, not to mention the £60,000 spent on the gorgeous blooms decorating the ceremony, including Holly's modern bouquet of 'black magic' calla lilies.

    Thirty-nine-year-old Nick, who has an estimated personal fortune of £155 million, really pulled out all the stops to ensure Holly had the wedding of her dreams.
