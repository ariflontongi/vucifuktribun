Jim Varney Net Worth

Jim Varney was an American comedic actor born on June 15, 1949 in Lexington, KY. He was best known for his portrayal of Ernest Worrell in the franchise based around the character. He also had roles in The Beverly Hillbillies and the Toy Story films, where he played the role of Slinky. Varney was a beloved actor who left a lasting legacy in the entertainment industry.

About

Comedic actor who became known for portraying Ernest Worrell in the franchise based around the character. He also appeared in The Beverly Hillbillies and portrayed the role of Slinky in the Toy Story films.

Trivia
