    If you're wondering how to style faux leather trousers, it all depends on the style of the trousers themselves. A five-pocket style is just like a pair of jeans - wear it with a blouse, a knit top or a t-shirt or button-down with a blazer. Faux leather leggings with no pockets look great layered and under tighter knits, so there will be no bumps showing. You can also wear them with a menswear-style shirt or chunky knit for contrast.
