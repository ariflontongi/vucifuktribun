Made in Chelsea cast wrap up filming in New York after two months

    Lucy Watson and the rest of the Made in Chelsea cast are on cloud nine after wrapping up filming the spin-off show .On Sunday Lucy joyfully tweeted that she would soon reunite with her pet dog after being away for two months. "Going home today and now there's only a flight and a 3 hour drive in between Digby and I. Too excited."

    Lucy Watson and Stevie Johnson have been filming together in New York

    She then wrote: "New York has been cray but I am so ready to come home. See you soon London (and Digby) x"Lucy has been in the Big Apple since May with co-stars Jamie Laing, Binky Felstead, Stevie Johnson, Rosie Fortescue, Louise Thompson, Oliver Proudlock, Spencer Matthews, Fran Newman-Young and Mark-Francis Vandelli.

    Lucy Watson: "Perfect way to finish."

     The trailer for the spin-off, which was released on 14 July, gives a glimpse of what will be included in the upcoming episodes.The 45-second clip features several cast members who reveal there will be "awkward dates", "ridiculous party themes" and "randomly bumping into each other...a lot".The spin-off will be aired in August and will consist of six episodes and reveal how the well-groomed "rahs" take the Big Apple by storm.
