How does the gloom sword work?

The Gloom Sword is a powerful one-handed weapon in the game. It has a unique ability to infect Link with gloom with every swing he makes. The sword is said to end the life of anyone it touches and its gloom will gradually wear down the body of its wielder. However, it can be used carefully as it can also be fused with other weapons or materials to nullify its negative effects or increase its durability.

Are the gloom weapons worth using?

They do good damage but they have low durability and can break easily. It is recommended to use them early in the game and not hoard them.

What should I fuse the Gloom Sword with?

There are a few ways to counter the negative effects of the Gloom Sword. One way is by fusing it with the base of another weapon to nullify its effects, although the Gloom Sword will break off after its durability runs out. Another way is by fusing the Gloom Sword with a Light Dragon Part, which allows the wielder to heal themselves when using the sword.

Can the Gloom Sword break?

The Gloom Sword itself does not break, but if it is used as a fuse material for another weapon, it will lose its Gloom Toll passive ability and break off once the base weapon’s durability runs out.
