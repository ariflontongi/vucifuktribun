Meet the cast of Garden Rescue 2019 who is the new presenter Arit Anderson? Tue 7 May 2019

The brand new series of Garden Rescue is already underway on BBC One, with the show spreading some much-needed garden magic up and down the country.

Airing every weekday at 15:45 pm, the brand new series has already got viewers hooked for its amazing landscape transformations and garden overhauls.

BBC Garden Rescue

Charlie Dimmock

What would BBC‘s Garden Rescue be without long-time TV host and gardener extraordinaire Charlie Dimmock?

Having appeared on loads of shows over the years including Ground Force, Charlie’s Garden Army and The Chelsea Flower Show, it’s clear to see that Charlie is a gardening expert.

You can follow her on Twitter at @Charlie_Dimmock where she has over 5,000 followers

Arit Anderson

The brand new series of Garden Rescue has welcomed a brand new presenter, award-winning garden designer Arit Anderson.

Alongside her work as a host on Garden Rescue, she also works with Gardeners World and even appeared on the front cover of their magazine this April!

You can follow her on Twitter at @diamondhill2012 where she has 1.6k followers.

The Rich brothers

Along with Charlie, the lovely Rich brothers, Harry and David have returned to the BBC for series 4 of Garden Rescue.

When they aren’t presenting the show, the family duo is busy working as award-winning landscape designers, transforming gardens up and down the country.

You can follow them on Twitter at @therichbrothers where they have over 6,000 followers.
